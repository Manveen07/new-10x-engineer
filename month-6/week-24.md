## Why this week matters

The plan's window closes — but this is a checkpoint, not a cliff. If interviews are live, this week is buffer: prep, follow up, negotiate any trial. If not, the move is to extend the funnel into Nov–Dec with more volume, not to declare failure. Either way you finish with the full readiness bar in hand: three deployed eval-backed projects, an MCP server, a merged OSS PR, six posts, and an open funnel.

**The single must-do:** every active conversation moved forward (reply, schedule, or close) + an honest readiness self-assessment against the bar — by Sunday.

Can you explain each in <1 min: error analysis, judge calibration, RAG-vs-long-context, hybrid+rerank, workflow-vs-agent, trajectory eval, cost-per-task, schema-as-eval-spec?

Set a Nov–Dec cadence: 50 outbound/week + continued applications + iterate on whatever isn't converting (the funnel is still an eval problem).
